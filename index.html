The Philosophy

Traditional systems are broken.

Most apps ask you to confirm your presence every single day. That's 40+ clicks a month just to say "I'm here."

We assume you are present by default. You only open the app when you skip. Reducing effort to just ~2 clicks a month.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the skip-first model?
It's highly accurate because it tracks your progress in real-time. By assuming you're present and only requiring input when you skip, we capture your data with zero friction.
What if I don't know the total number of classes?
You can estimate the total classes for the semester, and our engine will calculate your "Skip Budget" based on that. You can update the total classes anytime in the subject settings.
Can I track multiple subjects?
Absolutely. Add as many subjects as you need, each with its own attendance threshold, recovery plan, and personalized notes.
